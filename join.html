Engaging: Study Tours and Ministry Connections

“For the earth will be filled with the knowledge of the glory of the Lord, as the waters cover the sea." (Habakkuk 2:14, NASB 95)

Jamie Hulker: Here for You is not just a study of God's Word—it's a call to embody the truths we uncover. Through our partner ministry, The Uncommon Journey, we offer unique, transformative experiences to deepen your walk with God as you serve Him by serving others. We call these opportunities "Walking in the Footsteps" and "Walking Alongside." We invite you to journey with us beyond the pages of the Bible and live out its lessons in meaningful, impactful ways.

Walking in the Footsteps: Bible Study Tours

Walking in the Footsteps is like experiencing the Bible in 3D! We believe that immersing yourself in the very places where biblical events unfolded, guided by local experts who offer rich cultural, historical insights, and our own Study tour leaders’ biblical teaching, can profoundly deepen your understanding of Scripture. We offer two extraordinary journeys detailed below. To explore these transformative experiences in greater depth, email us at: jamie@theuncommonjourney.net

An Uncommon Journey to Israel

Join us as we follow in Jesus' footsteps, exploring the land where the stories you've studied come to life. We will travel together through the Galilee and Dead Sea regions, with stops in places such as Caesarea Maritima, Mt. Carmel, Megiddo, Capernaum, Masada, and En Gedi. We will even have opportunities to sail on the Sea of Galilee, be baptized in the Jordan River, and float in the Dead Sea! We will end our incredible journey together in Jerusalem, where we will visit The Garden of Gethsemane, The Temple Mount and Western Wall, and the Garden Tomb, among other stops. We will even walk on a street where Jesus walked! Our next trip is scheduled for November 5-15, 2025.

This trip is scheduled for November 5-15, 2025.*

Footsteps of Paul

Follow in the Apostle Paul's footsteps, discovering the places where Paul and the early Christians lived and ministered. We will begin our trip in Greece, including stops in places such as Athens, Corinth, Thessaloniki, and Philippi. We will then head to Ephesus and Izmir in Turkey and end our time together in the Eternal City-Rome!

The Uncommon Journey: Walking Alongside

The Uncommon Journey: Walking Alongside ministry provides meaningful opportunities for you to be a part of God's work worldwide. This aspect of our ministry is carried out with our long-time, well-established international ministry partners. Currently, we have partnerships in:

Zagreb, Croatia (with Domigoj and Bibi)

Rafiki Children’s Home-Jos, Nigeria (with Grace)

We also encourage you to explore other ministries that align with your interests. If you need recommendations, let us know! We would love to connect you with a place where you can utilize your God-given design and giftings! Email us at jamie@theuncommonjourney.net

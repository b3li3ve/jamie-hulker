Engage

“…Then I said, “Here am I. Send! me!” (Isaiah 6:8)

Jamie Hulker: Here for You is not just a study of God's Word—it's a call to embody the truths we uncover. Through "The Uncommon Journey," we offer unique, transformative experiences designed to deepen your connection with Scripture. These immersive opportunities, known as "Walking in the Footsteps" and "Walking Alongside," invite you to journey beyond the pages of the Bible and live out its lessons in meaningful, impactful ways.

Walking in the Footsteps: Bible Study Tours

Walking in the Footsteps is like experiencing the Bible in 3D! We believe that immersing yourself in the very places where biblical events unfolded, guided by local experts who offer rich cultural, historical insights, and our own Study tour leaders’ biblical teaching, can profoundly deepen your understanding of Scripture. We offer two extraordinary journeys detailed below. To explore these transformative experiences in greater depth, email Jamie at: jamiehulker@theuncommonjourney.net

Israel 2025

Join us as we walk in the footsteps of Jesus, exploring the land where the stories you've studied came to life. Our next trip is scheduled for November 5-15, 2025.

*Note: This trip is still scheduled; however, if Israel remains embattled after January 30, 2025, we will postpone until November 6-16, 2026.*

Footsteps of Paul

Follow in the Apostle Paul's footsteps, discovering the places where Paul and the early Christians lived and ministered.

Stay tuned for more details! This trip is currently scheduled for 2026, but if we need to postpone Israel 2025, we'll reschedule this trip for either 2025 or 2027, depending on interest.

Walking Alongside

Walking Alongside offers profound opportunities to engage directly in God’s work across the globe. This facet of our ministry is carried out in collaboration with our long-standing, reputable international partners. Through these meaningful partnerships, we provide impactful support and share in the mission of advancing God's Kingdom. Currently, we are honored to partner with:

Fokus-Zagreb, Croatia (with Domigoj and Bibi)

Rafiki Children’s Home-Jos, Nigeria (with Grace)

We also encourage you to explore additional global and national ministries that align with your interests and spiritual gifts. We maintain a curated list of recommended partners across various regions. If you need guidance or have questions, please don’t hesitate to reach out to us at: jamiehulker@theuncommonjourney.net

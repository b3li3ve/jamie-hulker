Engaging: Study Tours and Ministry Connections

“For the earth will be filled with the knowledge of the glory of the Lord, as the waters cover the sea." (Habakkuk 2:14, NASB 95)

Jamie Hulker: Here for You isn’t just about studying God’s Word—it’s about living it out. Through our partner ministry, The Uncommon Journey, we offer life-changing opportunities to grow deeper in your faith while serving others. We call these experiences 'Walking in the Footsteps' and 'Walking Alongside,' and we’d love for you to join us as we take what we’ve learned beyond the pages of the Bible and make an impact together.

Walking in the Footsteps: Bible Study Tours

Walking in the Footsteps brings the Bible to life! Explore the very places where biblical events happened, guided by local experts and our own study leaders. These immersive journeys will deepen your understanding of Scripture in powerful ways. For more info, check out the details below or email us at  jamie@theuncommonjourney.net!

An Uncommon Journey to Israel

Join us as we follow in Jesus' footsteps, exploring the land where the stories you've studied will come to life before your very eyes!

We will travel together through the Galilee and Dead Sea regions, stopping at places such as Caesarea Maritima, Mt. Carmel, Megiddo, Capernaum, Masada, and En Gedi. We will even have opportunities to sail on the Sea of Galilee, be baptized in the Jordan River, and even float in the Dead Sea!

We will end our incredible journey together in Jerusalem, where we will visit, among other stops, The Garden of Gethsemane, The Temple Mount and Western Wall, and the Garden Tomb. We will even walk on a street where Jesus walked!

Trip Details

  • When: November 5-15, 2025
  • Estimated Cost: $3324 (not including airfare). This includes all tours, in-country transportation, tips, lodging, and some meals.

Footsteps of Paul

We invite you to join us as we follow in the Apostle Paul's footsteps, discovering where Paul and the early Christians lived and ministered. Our Trip will begin in Athens, Greece, and will include stops in places such as Corinth, Thessaloniki, and Philippi. We will then head to Turkey, where we will take in the magnificent ruins of Ephesus, and we’ll end our journey together in the Eternal City-Rome!

The Uncommon Journey: Walking Alongside

The Uncommon Journey: Walking Alongside provides two meaningful and impactful ways for you to be a part of God's work worldwide—going and praying—alongside our long-time international ministry partners.

Zagreb, Croatia (with Domigoj and Bibi)

Fokus’ vision aligns closely with our own—serving God by serving the people of Croatia. Their heart is to make a lasting impact by meeting the spiritual and practical needs of Croatians, all for the glory of God. Through dedicated service, they are walking out their mission to bring hope, encouragement, and the transformative love of Christ to those they serve.

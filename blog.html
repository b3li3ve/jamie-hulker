Encourage

“Having so fond an affection for you, we were well-pleased to impart to you not only the gospel of God but also our own lives…” (1 Thess. 1:8a)

Our goal is not only to provide opportunities to study God’s eternal, life-changing Word but also to share what He is doing in our own lives because we believe that being connected and sharing life together is essential to our walk with Jesus. Seeing how God is working in the lives of other believers—what He’s teaching them and how He’s moving in their lives—is a wonderful way to learn more about Who He is and His love for us. We seek to foster this connection through our blog and occasional events. While we definitely encourage you to find this community within your local church, we recognize it’s not always readily available. Whatever your situation, we invite you to join our community!

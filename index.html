Welcome to Jamie Hulker: Here for You

Where our mission is to serve God by serving His people. We desire to help make the rich truths of God's Word accessible and understandable for everyone, so we are dedicated to equipping Christians with resources for discipleship and growth that engage both hearts and minds. Whether you're new to this journey or have been walking with the Lord for years, we're here for you!

About Jamie

Jamie discovered the importance of God's Word, prayer, and Christian community during her college years. Her journey has since taken her to over thirty countries across six continents, where she has served others through evangelism, writing and teaching Bible studies, and various other ministries to encourage spiritual growth. With a deep passion for studying and teaching the Bible from its historical and literary settings, Jamie earned an MA in Ministry from Moody Theological Seminary. Afterward, she founded Jamie Hulker: Here for You to leverage her seasoned experience and education to serve God by equipping and encouraging others to know Him better and follow Him more closely. Jamie lives in Franklin, Tennessee, where she loves to teach, speak, and connect with her community.

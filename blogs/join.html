Engage

“…Then I said, “Here am I. Send! me!” (Isaiah 6:8)

Jamie Hulker: Here for you is more than a study of God’s Word; it’s a call to live out the lessons we learn. We offer two uncommon ways to enhance your understanding of the Bible through immersive experiences through “the Uncommon Journey.” We call these opportunities: “Walking in the Footsteps” and “Walking Alongside.”

Walking in the Footsteps: Bible Study Tours

Walking in the Footsteps is like a 3D Bible study! We believe that immersing yourself in the places where the Bible was written and learning from local guides about their culture and history can profoundly deepen your biblical understanding. We offer two remarkable journeys, which you can learn more about by emailing Jamie at: jamiehulker@theuncommonjourney.net

Israel 2025

Join us as we walk in the footsteps of Jesus, exploring the land where the stories you've studied came to life. Our next trip is scheduled for November 5-15, 2025.

Note: This trip is still scheduled; however, if Israel remains embattled after January 30, 2025, we will postpone until November 6-16, 2026.

Footsteps of Paul

Follow in the Apostle Paul's footsteps, discovering the places where Paul and the early Christians lived and ministered.

Stay tuned for more details! This trip is currently scheduled for 2026, but if we need to postpone Israel 2025, we'll reschedule this trip for either 2025 or 2027, depending on interest.

Walking Alongside

Walking Alongside provides meaningful opportunities to be a part of God's work around the world. This aspect of our ministry is carried out in partnership with our long-time, well-established international ministry partners. Currently, we have partnerships with:

  • Fokus-Zagreb, Croatia
  • Rafiki Children’s Home-Jos, Nigeria

We also encourage you to explore other national ministries that align with your interests:

  • Charity Water
  • GraceWorks Ministries
